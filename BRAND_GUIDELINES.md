# Aster Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

Aster is a production-oriented Apple Silicon local LLM inference runtime. Our brand reflects innovation, efficiency, and professionalism.

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The Aster logo is an 8-pointed star with a purple-to-indigo gradient, representing:
- **Star**: The name "Aster" (Latin for star)
- **Gradient**: High-tech innovation and AI
- **Glow**: Speed and efficiency of inference
- **Accent Lines**: Neural networks and AI processing

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size**: 32x32px
- **Clear Space**: Maintain at least 10px of clear space around the logo
- **Background**: Works best on white or light backgrounds
- **Scaling**: Always scale proportionally

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary**: Inter, Helvetica, or system sans-serif
- **Fallback**: Arial, sans-serif

### Font Weights
- **Regular**: 400 - Body text
- **Medium**: 500 - Emphasis
- **Semibold**: 600 - Headings
- **Bold**: 700 - Strong emphasis

### Font Sizes
- **H1**: 32px, Semibold
- **H2**: 24px, Semibold
- **H3**: 20px, Semibold
- **Body**: 16px, Regular
- **Small**: 14px, Regular
- **Caption**: 12px, Regular

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional**: Credible and trustworthy
- **Innovative**: Forward-thinking and cutting-edge
- **Approachable**: Friendly and accessible
- **Clear**: Direct and easy to understand

### Tone Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Be concise and direct
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Use inclusive language
- Be helpful and supportive

## Visual Style

### Design Principles
1. **Minimalism**: Clean, uncluttered design
2. **Consistency**: Uniform visual language
3. **Accessibility**: Clear, readable, and inclusive
4. **Performance**: Fast-loading, optimized assets
5. **Professionalism**: Enterprise-grade quality

### Design Elements
- **Gradients**: Use the primary color gradient for emphasis
- **Icons**: Simple, geometric shapes
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace for clarity
- **Shadows**: Subtle, not overwhelming
- **Borders**: Thin, light gray (#e0e7ff)

## Photography & Imagery

### Style
- Modern, clean aesthetic
- Technology-focused
- Diverse and inclusive
- High quality and professional

### Guidelines
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1200px wide)
- Maintain consistent color grading
- Avoid stock photos that look generic
- Ensure images support the message

## Dos and Don'ts

### Do's ✅
- Use the official logo
- Maintain brand colors
- Keep design clean and simple
- Use consistent typography
- Provide adequate whitespace
- Test on multiple devices
- Update assets regularly

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't distort the logo
- Don't change brand colors without reason
- Don't use outdated assets
- Don't mix fonts inconsistently
- Don't overcrowd designs
- Don't use low-quality images
- Don't ignore accessibility
